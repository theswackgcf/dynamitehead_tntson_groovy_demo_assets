## Welcome to the DYNAMITEHEAD TNTSON (The Groovy Demo)'s assets repository!

Here you'll find .fla files for all in-game graphics.

In order to open said assets, you'll need a spare copy of **Macromedia Flash 8**. You can also use any version of **Adobe Flash** and older versions of **Adobe Animate**.

It's important to note that most of the in-game assets are animated in **18 fps**. Keep this in mind when making your own graphics! Once imported into the engine, resize the sprite to be **60%** of its original size (Using linear interpolation).
